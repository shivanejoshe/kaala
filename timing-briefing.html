Questions

Is this astrology?

The computation is astronomical. Swiss Ephemeris calculates planetary positions with sub-arcsecond precision using orbital mechanics — not beliefs. The Vimshottari dasha system is a mathematical structure: given a birth moment, the entire period sequence for a lifetime is deterministic. The interpretation layer translates that computation into plain language about timing.

How is this different from other astrology apps?

Most apps give you generic Sun-sign content. Kaala uses your exact birth data to compute a chart unique to you — your specific planetary positions, your ascendant, your dasha cycle. The interpretive engine reads 2,000–10,000 individual computations from your chart to produce a reading that applies specifically to you, not to everyone born in the same month.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

Use your best estimate. Birth times within 30 minutes of the actual time produce accurate dasha calculations. The ascendant is more sensitive to time — if you're more than an hour off, the house placements may shift. For most people, the dasha timing (which drives the core of your briefing) is accurate within a 1-2 hour window.

Can I ask follow-up questions?

Yes. After your briefing, you have 5 follow-up questions — ask about a specific decision, a relationship, a career window, a move. The chart is saved and the engine responds from the same computed data as your briefing.
