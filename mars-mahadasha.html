Vimshottari Dasha · 7 Years

Mars Mahadasha

Mars Mahadasha runs for 7 years. Whether it delivers ambition, conflict, property, career breaks, or something else entirely depends on one thing: your ascendant, and where Mars sits in your specific chart.

What Mars represents

Mars rules action, ambition, physical energy, siblings, property, engineering, the military, and direct confrontation. It is a natural malefic — not in the sense of being bad, but in the sense that it acts by cutting through, forcing things to a head, and creating pressure that demands response.

A well-placed Mars in a strong period delivers: career acceleration, property acquisition, decisive moves that pay off, physical vitality, and the ability to execute quickly. A poorly-placed or afflicted Mars delivers: accidents, surgeries, conflict with authorities, disputes over property or money, relationships under pressure, and decisions made in anger that cost later.

Mars never makes you passive. A Mars period is always a period of action — the question is whether that action is directed or scattered.

Mars by ascendant — what this period delivers for you

The same 7 years behaves fundamentally differently depending on your rising sign. Mars lords different houses for each ascendant. Those house lordships determine whether Mars is functionally benefic, malefic, or mixed in your chart.

Aries ascendant

Strong — with complications

Mars is the chart ruler. Strong identity, career push, leadership. Mars also lords the 8th — sudden events, health, hidden opponents. A powerful but unpredictable period.

Taurus ascendant

Mixed — friction and gain

Mars lords the 7th (partnerships) and 12th (loss, foreign). Career and relationship pressure. Possible gains through foreign work. Hidden expenses.

Gemini ascendant

Difficult

Mars lords the 6th (enemies, health, debt) and 11th. A functional malefic. Friction, disputes, competition. Gains possible but through struggle.

Cancer ascendant

Excellent — Yogakaraka

Mars lords the 5th (intelligence, creativity) and 10th (career). A Yogakaraka — the single most benefic planet for Cancer. Career peaks, recognition, family fortune.

Leo ascendant

Excellent — Yogakaraka

Mars lords the 4th (home, property) and 9th (fortune, dharma). Yogakaraka for Leo. Property acquisition, fortune, father's support, dharmic clarity.

Virgo ascendant

Mixed — functional malefic

Mars lords the 3rd and 8th. Effort, sibling dynamics, health challenges. Mars is a functional malefic here — period requires discipline to navigate.

Libra ascendant

Difficult — maraka

Mars lords the 2nd (family, speech, wealth) and 7th (spouse, partnerships). A maraka lord. Health caution, relationship pressure, financial restructuring.

Scorpio ascendant

Good — chart ruler

Mars is the chart ruler (1st) and lords the 6th. Chart ruler periods are generally productive. Watch for conflict and overexertion — Mars in 6th also means competitive friction.

Sagittarius ascendant

Mixed

Mars lords 5th and 12th. Creative intelligence and hidden losses. Study, speculation, children, and expenses abroad. Outcomes depend heavily on Mars's placement.

Capricorn ascendant

Mixed — functional malefic

Mars lords 4th and 11th. Property gains possible, income through networks. But Mars is not a benefic here — gains come with friction and pressure.

Aquarius ascendant

Difficult

Mars lords 3rd and 10th. Career pressure, authority conflict, sibling disputes. Functional malefic. Ambition is high but resistance is also high.

Pisces ascendant

Good — trine lord

Mars lords 2nd and 9th. Wealth, fortune, dharma. A trine lord with good placement delivers financial growth and philosophical clarity. Watch speech and family dynamics (2nd lordship).

Important

The ascendant analysis above is only the starting point. Where Mars is placed in your chart, what planets it's associated with or aspected by, and its nakshatra position all modify the delivery significantly.

Two people with the same ascendant in a Mars Mahadasha can have entirely different 7-year experiences if one has Mars exalted in Capricorn in the 10th house and the other has Mars debilitated in Cancer in the 4th. The ascendant is a starting point. The full chart is what determines the outcome.

The nine sub-periods within Mars Mahadasha

The 7-year Mars Mahadasha is subdivided into nine Antardashas. Their sequence and durations:

Mars / Mars

~4.5 months. The period begins intensely — pure Mars energy. Ambition, action, possible impulsiveness. Decisions made here set the tone for the full 7 years.

Mars / Rahu

~12 months. The longest and often the most turbulent sub-period. Rahu amplifies Mars — ambition spikes, foreign connections, obsessive drives. Also the period most associated with accidents or sudden events. Proceed deliberately.

Mars / Jupiter

~11 months. The most favorable sub-period for most charts. Jupiter expands and blesses Mars's energy. Career recognition, spiritual growth, legal matters resolve favorably.

Mars / Saturn

~11 months. A period of structured effort. Results come but slowly and through sustained work. Delays are common but the groundwork laid here is durable.

Mars / Mercury

~11 months. Business, communication, analytical work, education. Good for entrepreneurial ventures and skill-building.

Mars / Ketu

~4.5 months. Ketu brings detachment and sudden separations. Past patterns surface. Spiritual dimensions activate. Can feel disorienting — a pivot point in the period.

Mars / Venus

~12 months. Relationships, creative work, finance, comfort. A more pleasurable sub-period within an otherwise intense Mahadasha. Good for marriage, artistic work, and material acquisition.

Mars / Sun

~4 months. Authority, government, career recognition. Father's health or relationship may come into focus. A brief but significant window.

Mars / Moon

~7 months. The period closes with Moon's energy — emotions, mind, public, mother. A more introspective close to the Mahadasha, often involving relocation or domestic changes.
