How It Works
Layer 1

Astronomical Signals

Swiss Ephemeris (sub-arc-minute precision). Tracks Mercury retrograde, planetary stations, Saturn near natal sensitive axis, Sun transit, Mars transit, eclipse corridors — all computed against India (1947), BSE (1875), NSE (1994) founding charts.

Layer 2

Panchang Rules

Five Vedic timing factors computed daily: Tithi (lunar day), Nakshatra quality (Tikshna/Mridu/Kshipra), Yoga (Vyatipata/Vaidhriti), Vara (weekday), Karana (Bhadra). Backtested — Rikta tithi shows 1.28× lift at market peaks.

Layer 3

Calibrated Weights

Signal weights are derived from backtest lift ratios — not theoretical Siddhanta weights. Each signal's weight = its empirical lift at peaks (caution model) or troughs (recovery model). Separating the two models eliminates the noise that averaging creates.

Not financial advice. This is a research tool applying temporal pattern analysis to market data. Lift ratios of 1.1–1.4× indicate statistical tendency, not certainty. Signals have predictive value as one input among many — not as standalone trade triggers. Backtest covers 2007–2026 (19 years, 66 turning points) — sample size is sufficient for direction but not for high-confidence magnitude predictions.
